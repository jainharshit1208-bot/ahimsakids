DISCOVER THEM ALL

MEET THE 24 TIRTHANKARAS ✨

1
🐂
Rishabhdev (Adinath)
Birthplace: Ayodhya
Taught humanity the first arts of civilization, agriculture, and cooking.
He is considered the first Tirthankara and king!
2
🐘
Ajitanath
Birthplace: Ayodhya
Taught that true victory is over oneself, not others.
His name means 'unconquerable'!
3
🐴
Sambhavanath
Birthplace: Shravasti
Taught patience and self-control as paths to liberation.
He practiced austerities for 14 years!
4
🐒
Abhinandannath
Birthplace: Ayodhya
Taught that joy comes from within, not from material things.
His name means 'one who brings joy'!
5
🦅
Sumatinath
Birthplace: Ayodhya
Taught wisdom and good counsel to all beings.
His name means 'one with a good mind'!
6
🪷
Padmaprabha
Birthplace: Kaushambi
Taught purity of heart, like a lotus rising from mud.
His body glowed like a red lotus!
7
✡️
Suparshvanath
Birthplace: Varanasi
Taught auspiciousness and right conduct.
He had a swastika symbol, meaning well-being!
8
🌙
Chandraprabha
Birthplace: Chandrapuri
Taught that inner light dispels all darkness.
His body glowed like the moon!
9
🐊
Pushpadanta (Suvidhinath)
Birthplace: Kakandi
Taught proper conduct and righteous living.
He is also known as Suvidhinath!
10
🌳
Sheetalnath
Birthplace: Bhaddilapura
Taught calmness and coolness of mind.
His name means 'the cool one' — he brought peace!
11
🦏
Shreyansnath
Birthplace: Simhapuri
Taught seeking the good and noble path.
His name means 'one who seeks the best'!
12
🦬
Vasupujya
Birthplace: Champapuri
Taught worship of true wealth — knowledge and virtue.
He lived a royal life before renouncing it all!
13
🐗
Vimalanath
Birthplace: Kampilya
Taught purity and cleanliness of body, mind, and soul.
His name means 'the pure one'!
14
🦔
Anantanath
Birthplace: Ayodhya
Taught the infinite nature of the soul.
His name means 'the infinite one'!
15
⚡
Dharmanath
Birthplace: Ratnapuri
Taught that dharma (righteousness) is the true support.
He was known for his unwavering commitment to truth!
16
🦌
Shantinath
Birthplace: Hastinapura
Taught peace as the highest goal of life.
He was a great king (Chakravarti) before becoming a monk!
17
🐐
Kunthunath
Birthplace: Hastinapura
Taught humility and surrender of ego.
He was also a Chakravarti (universal monarch)!
18
🐟
Aranath
Birthplace: Hastinapura
Taught liberation through detachment.
He was the third Tirthankara who was also a Chakravarti!
19
🏺
Mallinath
Birthplace: Mithila
Taught equality and that the soul has no gender.
According to Shvetambar tradition, Mallinath was female!
20
🐢
Munisuvrata
Birthplace: Rajgriha
Taught silence, meditation, and inner peace.
His name means 'one with a sage's vows'!
21
🪷
Naminath
Birthplace: Mithila
Taught bowing to truth and humility.
He was related to Lord Krishna's family!
22
🐚
Neminath (Arishtanemi)
Birthplace: Shauripur (Dwarka)
Taught compassion for all animals and non-violence.
He was cousin of Lord Krishna and refused to marry when he saw animals being killed for his wedding feast!
23
🐍
Parshvanath
Birthplace: Varanasi
Taught the four vows: Ahimsa, Truth, Non-stealing, Non-attachment.
A cobra spread its hood to protect him during meditation!
24
🦁
Mahavir (Vardhamana)
Birthplace: Kundalpur (Vaishali)
Taught the complete path to liberation through Ahimsa, Truth, Non-stealing, Celibacy, and Non-attachment.
He is the most recent Tirthankara, born around 599 BCE, and gave us the Panch Mahavrats!

CELEBRATE TOGETHER

JAIN FESTIVALS & KALYANAKS 🎊

Major Festival
MAHAVIR JAYANTI
Celebrates the birth of Lord Mahavir, the 24th Tirthankara. Grand processions, temple worship, and charity mark this joyous day. Falls in March-April.
🙏
🎉
🌸
8-Day Festival
PARYUSHAN
The most important Jain festival — 8 days of self-reflection, fasting, prayer, and forgiveness. Ends with the powerful Kshamavani day.
📿
🕊️
🙏
Forgiveness Day
KSHAMAVANI
A beautiful day when everyone asks for forgiveness saying 'Micchami Dukkadam' — 'May my wrong actions be forgiven.'
💛
🤝
✨
Kalyanak
JANMA KALYANAK
Celebrates the auspicious birth of a Tirthankara. Indra himself performs the Abhishek ceremony of the newborn.
👶
🌟
🎊
Kalyanak
DIKSHA KALYANAK
Celebrates when a Tirthankara renounces worldly life and begins the spiritual journey toward Kevalgyan.
🧘
✨
📿
Kalyanak
KEVALGYAN KALYANAK
Celebrates when a Tirthankara attains infinite knowledge — the ultimate spiritual awakening.
🌟
